Funding a better future

At LUX, we believe in the power of collective action and the potential of innovative projects. We have created a revolutionary crowdfunding platform that opens up a world of opportunities for both project creators and supporters. Our unique twist? We embrace the transformative potential of cryptocurrency.

Why choose us?

  • Crypto Compatibility
    Say goodbye to traditional payment methods and embrace the decentralized world. Lux seamlessly integrates with Freighter, allowing you to contribute without the bureaucracy of opening an account.
  • Global Reach
    We break down geographical barriers and connect project creators with a global community of backers. No matter where you are in the world, you can support projects that align with your passions and values.
  • Guaranteed Funding Approach
    With Lux, backing a project is risk-free. If a project doesn't reach its funding goal, the backers get their contributions returned. This ensures that your money is committed only to projects with strong community support and a clear path to success.
  • Transparency and Accountability
    We foster a culture of transparency, where project creators are held accountable for their progress and backers can track the impact of their contributions. Our smart contract technology ensures that funds are allocated appropriately and project milestones are met.
  • Diverse Project Portfolio
    Lux hosts a diverse range of projects across various sectors, including technology, books, games and more. Explore exciting ventures, support groundbreaking innovations, and be a part of shaping the future.
  • Enhanced Security
    Our platform prioritizes the highest standards of security and encryption protocols, ensuring that your crypto assets remain safe throughout the donation process. You can have peace of mind knowing that your contributions are protected.
