How it works

  • How do I create a wallet?
    To start using our platform, you need to create a digital wallet through the Freighter extension. Hereâ€™s how to set it up:
    1. Install the Freighter's extension in your browser.
    2. Open the extension and follow the instructions to create a new wallet. Make sure to save the 12-word recovery phrase securely. This phrase allows you to access your wallet from any browser.
    3. Click on the 'Connect' button located at the top right corner of our platform.
    4. Select 'Connect with Freighter' from the options.
    5. Ensure you are on the TESTNET network.
    6. Fund it using the friendbot feature within the Freighter extension. Don't worry, it's not real money.
    Need a visual guide? Watch our video tutorial here.
  • How do I start my own project and how does it work?
    Starting your own crowdfunding project is simple. Here's what you need to do:
    1. Ensure your wallet is connected. Then, navigate to and click on 'New Project'.
    2. Complete the required fields with your project information. This includes a description, goals, and other essential details.
    3. Upload an image that represents your project. You will have the option to crop it for a perfect fit.
    4. Once your project is live, you can monitor its progress on its specific page. Here, some details can be edited if needed.
    5. If your project meets its funding goal by the deadline, the platform's admin account will transfer the collected lumens (XLM) to you.
    6. Should the project not reach its goal, all funds will be returned to the backers, ensuring a risk-free contribution.
    To manage and view all of your projects, visit the 'My Account' page.
  • How can I make a donation?
    Making a donation to projects on our platform is straightforward. Follow these steps to support your chosen project:
    1. First, make sure your Freighter wallet is connected to our platform. This is essential for processing your donation.
    2. Navigate to the projects page, where you can search by category, status, or name, and click on the chosen project.
    3. Enter the amount you wish to donate in the designated field on the project's specific page.
    4. Click the 'Donate' button. The Freighter extension will then prompt you for confirmation and your password.
    5. Once your donation is processed, you will receive a confirmation hash.
    If the project does not reach its funding goal, your donation will be refunded. To view and keep track of all your donations, click on 'My Account' on the navigation bar and select the 'My Donations' option.
  • How the admin account works?
    The admin account funds and refunds projects after they expire:
    1. Make sure you have inserted an admin public key in the .env file.
    2. After connecting your admin account wallet, go to the 'Control Panel' page, which can only be accessed by the admin account.
    3. Check the boxes of the projects to fund or refund and click on the respective button.
    4. Upon action, a transaction is constructed and displayed in a new modal. Once confirmed, the Freighter extension is activated to sign the transaction.
    5. The projects will have their status updated and can be seen on the 'All' tab from the Control Panel page.
    The admin account is restricted from donating to or launching its own projects. Attempts to do so will redirect the user to the 'Control Panel' page.
  • How the transactions work?
    Each Stellar transaction can include one or more operations. Lux is configured so that a donation constitutes a single operation within a transaction. However, when the admin funds or refunds multiple projects, a transaction may contain one or more operations, depending on the number of projects involved.
  • How the projects updates work?
    Every time someone runs the program, the status of expired projects is updated accordingly: to 'fund' if the project reached its goal amount, to 'refund' if it received donations but did not reach the final goal, and to 'unsuccessful' if it did not receive any donations. All the transactions and projects save instantly on a local database.
  • Can I cancel my project?
    Yes, you can cancel your project directly from its page, but this action is irreversible. Any received donations will be refunded to doners, and your project's status will be updated to 'refund', otherwise the status will change to 'unsuccessful'.
  • How do I run and test this project?
    You can run it locally. To start a project or make a donation, you need to create and fund a Freighter wallet first. To test the admin account, you need to add the public key into the configuration file. Check out the READ ME to learn how.
